About

We are a small recording studio located in Downtown Bastrop Texas. We specialize in vocal, guitar and piano tracking for almost any genre of music and we are just big enough to track a 4-piece drum set. We offer music beds, stingers, sweepers, voice overs and sound design. Just need mixing and mastering? We can do that too. We also offer classes on DJing in Serato and music production in Ableton Live.
Contact us through email or on our Instagram page for booking!
