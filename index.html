About

  • Location: Bastrop, Texas (SE of Austin)
  • Instagram: @goatsonlyrecordingstudio
  • Email: goatsonlyrecordingstudio@gmail.com

Located in the heart of downtown Bastrop, Goats Only Recording Studio is a professional yet intimate space designed for artists, podcasters, and creatives of all kinds. We specialize in recording vocals, guitar, and piano across nearly any genre, with just enough room to track a full 4-piece drum kit.

Our services include mixing, mastering, podcast editing, music beds, stingers, sweepers, voiceovers, and custom sound design to bring your projects to life.

Looking to sharpen your skills? We offer DJ classes (Serato) and music production courses (Ableton Live) to help you take your craft to the next level.

Whether you're capturing your next hit, refining your podcast, or exploring the world of music production, Goats Only Recording Studio is here to make it happen. Book a session today!
